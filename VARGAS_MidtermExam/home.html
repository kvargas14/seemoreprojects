SURVIVAL

The practice of protecting plant and animal species as well as their habitats is known as wildlife conservation. Wildlife, as a component of the world's ecosystems, contributes to the balance and stability of nature's processes. The goal of wildlife conservation is to ensure the survival of these species and to educate people on how to coexist with other species in a sustainable manner.
